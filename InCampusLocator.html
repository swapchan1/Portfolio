Overview

Problem Statement:

Any person, student or otherwise, new to the school campus feels lost. We wanted to focus on this problem space and help them find their way around the campus and inside buildings using technology.

Solution:

Our solution was a mobile application along with a kiosk on campus. The mobile application will guide the user through the campus and will guide the user inside the buildings through a road map. The users can also connect with their friends to find a route to the friend. This app can also be accessible on kiosks outside the school with a bigger screen size. Once the route the user requires is selected, it can be transferred to the app by connecting the two devices.

2. Persona

Based on the inputs by the students we created our personas.

  • Main Persona: Emma Williams, A freshman BS Student
  • Secondary Persona: Alex Miller, A graduate student

3. Mapping Methods

We conducted 2 methods to gain better understanding of the problems of the students:

  • Affinity Mapping
  • Concept Mapping

Using Affinity Mapping we were able to understand the problems the students faced in the above mentioned 4 areas and miscellaneous problems.

Using Concept Mapping we were able to understand how the problems were interconnected to one another and the 4 areas.

These methods helped us realize how large the scope of the project was and therefore we decided to narrow it down to just navigation around campus.

4. Brainstorm

Having our aim in mind, we brainstormed multiple possible solutions to find the most suitable one. We brainstormed 100 solutions out of which we decided upon 3 ideas :

  1. Mobile Application
  2. Augmented Reality
  3. Kiosk
